# **Translation Instructions for Self-Help Book: English to Natural Telugu**

## **YOUR MISSION**
Translate this English self-help book into **natural, flowing Telugu** that feels like a caring friend talking to you. Create a reading experience that is warm, encouraging, and effortlessly understood by everyday Telugu readers.

**Target audience:** Regular Telugu-speaking people in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana who read for personal growth—not scholars. They want simple wisdom in natural Telugu.

---

## **CRITICAL: CONSISTENCY IS NON-NEGOTIABLE**

**The reader should feel:**
"ఇది ఒక వ్యక్తి యొక్క స్వరం ఈ ప్రయాణంలో నాకు మార్గదర్శకత్వం చేస్తోంది."

### **Maintain Your Translation Decisions:**
1. **Terminology Consistency:** Track and reuse translations
2. **Pronoun Consistency:** Choose మీరు (formal) OR నువ్వు (informal)—never switch
3. **Tone Consistency:** Maintain warmth throughout

---

## **CRITICAL WARNING: AVOID TENGLISH TRAP**

**❌ TENGLISH (WRONG):**
"మీరు goals set చేయాలి మరియు daily routine లో discipline maintain చేయాలి।"

**✓ NATURAL TELUGU (CORRECT):**
"మీరు మీ లక్ష్యాలను నిర్ణయించుకోవాలి మరియు రోజువారీ జీవితంలో క్రమశిక్షణను పాటించాలి।"

### **The 90-10 Rule**
**90% Telugu, 10% English (only unavoidable terms)**

## **TELUGU LINGUISTIC NUANCES**

### **1. Avoid Excessive Sanskrit**
While Telugu has Sanskrit influence, use simpler Telugu when possible.

### **2. Proper Use of Telugu Letters**
Telugu has unique letters and combinations. Use correctly.

### **3. Natural Telugu Vocabulary**
- ప్రారంభం → ✓ ప్రారంభం / మొదలు (both acceptable)
- సమాప్తి → ✓ ముగింపు / సమాప్తి

---

## **TONE & VOICE: WARM AND ENCOURAGING**

**✓ DO:**
- Use మీరు for respectful warmth
- Sound encouraging: "మీరు దీన్ని ఖచ్చితంగా చేయగలరు!"
- Use rhetorical questions
- Be direct but kind

**❌ DON'T:**
- Sound preachy
- Use overly complex Sanskrit
- Pepper text with English words

---

## **FINAL PRINCIPLES**

1. **Use Telugu First** > Always default to Telugu
2. **Consistency Throughout** > Everything else
3. **Natural Flow** > Literal accuracy
4. **Minimize English to <5%** > Non-negotiable

**Create a Telugu self-help book that feels originally written in Telugu—warm, consistent, and free of unnecessary English.** 📖✨
