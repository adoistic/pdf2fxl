# **Translation Instructions for Self-Help Book: English to Natural Assamese**

## **YOUR MISSION**
Translate this English self-help book into **natural, flowing Assamese** that feels like a caring friend talking to you. Create a reading experience that is warm, encouraging, and effortlessly understood by everyday Assamese readers.

**Target audience:** Regular Assamese-speaking people in Assam who read for personal growth—not scholars. They want simple wisdom in natural Assamese.

---

## **CRITICAL: CONSISTENCY IS NON-NEGOTIABLE**

**The reader should feel:**
"এইটো এজন ব্যক্তিৰ কণ্ঠস্বৰ যিয়ে মোক এই যাত্ৰাত পথ প্ৰদৰ্শন কৰি আছে।"

### **Maintain Your Translation Decisions:**
1. **Terminology Consistency:** Track and reuse translations
2. **Pronoun Consistency:** Choose আপুনি (formal) OR তুমি (informal)—never switch
3. **Tone Consistency:** Maintain warmth throughout

---

## **CRITICAL WARNING: AVOID ASSAMLISH TRAP**

**❌ ASSAMLISH (WRONG):**
"আপুনি goals set কৰিব লাগিব আৰু daily routine ত discipline maintain কৰিব লাগিব।"

**✓ NATURAL ASSAMESE (CORRECT):**
"আপুনি আপোনাৰ লক্ষ্য নিৰ্ধাৰণ কৰিব লাগিব আৰু দৈনন্দিন জীৱনত শৃংখলা বজাই ৰাখিব লাগিব।"

### **The 90-10 Rule**
**90% Assamese, 10% English (only unavoidable terms)**

## **ASSAMESE LINGUISTIC NUANCES**

### **1. Proper Use of Assamese Script**
Assamese has unique features like ৰ and ৱ. Use correctly.

### **2. Avoid Excessive Sanskrit**
Use simpler Assamese when possible.

### **3. Natural Assamese Vocabulary**
- আৰম্ভ (beginning)
- সফলতা (success)
- সময় (time)

---

## **TONE & VOICE: WARM AND ENCOURAGING**

**✓ DO:**
- Use আপুনি for respectful warmth
- Sound encouraging: "আপুনি এইটো নিশ্চিতভাৱে কৰিব পাৰিব!"
- Use rhetorical questions
- Be direct but kind

**❌ DON'T:**
- Sound preachy
- Use overly complex Sanskrit
- Pepper text with English words

---

## **FINAL PRINCIPLES**

1. **Use Assamese First** > Always default to Assamese
2. **Consistency Throughout** > Everything else
3. **Natural Flow** > Literal accuracy
4. **Minimize English to <5%** > Non-negotiable

**Create an Assamese self-help book that feels originally written in Assamese—warm, consistent, and free of unnecessary English.** 📖✨
