# **Translation Instructions for Self-Help Book: English to Natural Kannada**

## **YOUR MISSION**
Translate this English self-help book into **natural, flowing Kannada** that feels like a caring friend talking to you. Create a reading experience that is warm, encouraging, and effortlessly understood by everyday Kannada readers.

**Target audience:** Regular Kannada-speaking people in Karnataka who read for personal growth—not scholars. They want simple wisdom in natural Kannada.

---

## **CRITICAL: CONSISTENCY IS NON-NEGOTIABLE**

**The reader should feel:**
"ಇದು ಒಬ್ಬ ವ್ಯಕ್ತಿಯ ಧ್ವನಿ ಈ ಪ್ರಯಾಣದಲ್ಲಿ ನನಗೆ ಮಾರ್ಗದರ್ಶನ ನೀಡುತ್ತಿದೆ."

### **Maintain Your Translation Decisions:**
1. **Terminology Consistency:** Track and reuse translations
2. **Pronoun Consistency:** Choose ನೀವು (formal) OR ನೀನು (informal)—never switch
3. **Tone Consistency:** Maintain warmth throughout

---

## **CRITICAL WARNING: AVOID KANGLISH TRAP**

**❌ KANGLISH (WRONG):**
"ನೀವು goals set ಮಾಡಬೇಕು ಮತ್ತು daily routine ನಲ್ಲಿ discipline maintain ಮಾಡಬೇಕು।"

**✓ NATURAL KANNADA (CORRECT):**
"ನೀವು ನಿಮ್ಮ ಗುರಿಗಳನ್ನು ನಿರ್ಧರಿಸಬೇಕು ಮತ್ತು ದೈನಂದಿನ ಜೀವನದಲ್ಲಿ ಶಿಸ್ತು ಕಾಪಾಡಬೇಕು।"

### **The 90-10 Rule**
**90% Kannada, 10% English (only unavoidable terms)**

## **KANNADA LINGUISTIC NUANCES**

### **1. Avoid Excessive Sanskrit**
While Kannada has Sanskrit influence, use simpler Kannada when possible.

### **2. Proper Use of ಳ (Retroflex L)**
Kannada has unique letters like ಳ. Use correctly.

### **3. Natural Kannada Vocabulary**
- ಪ್ರಾರಂಭ → ✓ ಆರಂಭ / ಪ್ರಾರಂಭ (both acceptable)
- ಸಮಾಪ್ತಿ → ✓ ಅಂತ್ಯ / ಮುಕ್ತಾಯ

---

## **TONE & VOICE: WARM AND ENCOURAGING**

**✓ DO:**
- Use ನೀವು for respectful warmth
- Sound encouraging: "ನೀವು ಇದನ್ನು ಖಂಡಿತವಾಗಿ ಮಾಡಬಹುದು!"
- Use rhetorical questions
- Be direct but kind

**❌ DON'T:**
- Sound preachy
- Use overly complex Sanskrit
- Pepper text with English words

---

## **FINAL PRINCIPLES**

1. **Use Kannada First** > Always default to Kannada
2. **Consistency Throughout** > Everything else
3. **Natural Flow** > Literal accuracy
4. **Minimize English to <5%** > Non-negotiable

**Create a Kannada self-help book that feels originally written in Kannada—warm, consistent, and free of unnecessary English.** 📖✨
