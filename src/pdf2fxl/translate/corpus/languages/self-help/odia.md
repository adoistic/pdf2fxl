# **Translation Instructions for Self-Help Book: English to Natural Odia**

## **YOUR MISSION**
Translate this English self-help book into **natural, flowing Odia** that feels like a caring friend talking to you. Create a reading experience that is warm, encouraging, and effortlessly understood by everyday Odia readers.

**Target audience:** Regular Odia-speaking people in Odisha who read for personal growth—not scholars. They want simple wisdom in natural Odia.

---

## **CRITICAL: CONSISTENCY IS NON-NEGOTIABLE**

**The reader should feel:**
"ଏହା ଜଣେ ବ୍ୟକ୍ତିଙ୍କ ସ୍ୱର ଯାହା ମୋତେ ଏହି ଯାତ୍ରାରେ ମାର୍ଗଦର୍ଶନ କରୁଛି।"

### **Maintain Your Translation Decisions:**
1. **Terminology Consistency:** Track and reuse translations
2. **Pronoun Consistency:** Choose ଆପଣ (formal) OR ତୁମେ (informal)—never switch
3. **Tone Consistency:** Maintain warmth throughout

---

## **CRITICAL WARNING: AVOID ODLISH TRAP**

**❌ ODLISH (WRONG):**
"ଆପଣଙ୍କୁ goals set କରିବାକୁ ପଡିବ ଏବଂ daily routine ରେ discipline maintain କରିବାକୁ ପଡିବ।"

**✓ NATURAL ODIA (CORRECT):**
"ଆପଣଙ୍କୁ ଆପଣଙ୍କର ଲକ୍ଷ୍ୟ ନିର୍ଧାରଣ କରିବାକୁ ପଡିବ ଏବଂ ଦୈନନ୍ଦିନ ଜୀବନରେ ଶୃଙ୍ଖଳା ବଜାୟ ରଖିବାକୁ ପଡିବ।"

### **The 90-10 Rule**
**90% Odia, 10% English (only unavoidable terms)**

## **ODIA LINGUISTIC NUANCES**

### **1. Avoid Excessive Sanskrit**
While Odia has Sanskrit influence, use simpler Odia when possible.

### **2. Proper Use of Odia Script**
Odia has unique script features. Use correctly.

### **3. Natural Odia Vocabulary**
- ପ୍ରାରମ୍ଭ → ✓ ଆରମ୍ଭ / ପ୍ରାରମ୍ଭ (both acceptable)
- ସମାପ୍ତି → ✓ ଶେଷ / ସମାପ୍ତି

---

## **TONE & VOICE: WARM AND ENCOURAGING**

**✓ DO:**
- Use ଆପଣ for respectful warmth
- Sound encouraging: "ଆପଣ ଏହା ନିଶ୍ଚିତ ଭାବରେ କରିପାରିବେ!"
- Use rhetorical questions
- Be direct but kind

**❌ DON'T:**
- Sound preachy
- Use overly complex Sanskrit
- Pepper text with English words

---

## **FINAL PRINCIPLES**

1. **Use Odia First** > Always default to Odia
2. **Consistency Throughout** > Everything else
3. **Natural Flow** > Literal accuracy
4. **Minimize English to <5%** > Non-negotiable

**Create an Odia self-help book that feels originally written in Odia—warm, consistent, and free of unnecessary English.** 📖✨
