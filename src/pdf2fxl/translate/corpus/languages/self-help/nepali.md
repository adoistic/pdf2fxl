# **Translation Instructions for Self-Help Book: English to Natural Nepali**

## **YOUR MISSION**
Translate this English self-help book into **natural, flowing Nepali** that feels like a caring friend talking to you. Create a reading experience that is warm, encouraging, and effortlessly understood by everyday Nepali readers.

**Target audience:** Regular Nepali-speaking people in Nepal who read for personal growth—not scholars. They want simple wisdom in natural Nepali.

---

## **CRITICAL: CONSISTENCY IS NON-NEGOTIABLE**

**The reader should feel:**
"यो एक व्यक्तिको आवाज हो जसले मलाई यो यात्रामा मार्गदर्शन गरिरहेको छ।"

### **Maintain Your Translation Decisions:**
1. **Terminology Consistency:** Track and reuse translations
2. **Pronoun Consistency:** Choose तपाईं (formal) OR तिमी (informal)—never switch
3. **Tone Consistency:** Maintain warmth throughout

---

## **CRITICAL WARNING: AVOID NEPLISH TRAP**

**❌ NEPLISH (WRONG):**
"तपाईंले goals set गर्नुपर्छ र daily routine मा discipline maintain गर्नुपर्छ।"

**✓ NATURAL NEPALI (CORRECT):**
"तपाईंले आफ्ना लक्ष्यहरू निर्धारण गर्नुपर्छ र दैनिक जीवनमा अनुशासन कायम राख्नुपर्छ।"

### **The 90-10 Rule**
**90% Nepali, 10% English (only unavoidable terms)**

## **NEPALI LINGUISTIC NUANCES**

### **1. Avoid Hindi Influence**
Nepali and Hindi are similar but different. Use authentic Nepali.

### **2. Proper Use of Devanagari**
Nepali uses Devanagari script. Use correctly.

### **3. Natural Nepali Vocabulary**
- सुरुवात (beginning)
- सफलता (success)
- समय (time)

---

## **TONE & VOICE: WARM AND ENCOURAGING**

**✓ DO:**
- Use तपाईं for respectful warmth
- Sound encouraging: "तपाईं यो पक्कै गर्न सक्नुहुन्छ!"
- Use rhetorical questions
- Be direct but kind

**❌ DON'T:**
- Sound preachy
- Use Hindi words when Nepali alternatives exist
- Pepper text with English words

---

## **FINAL PRINCIPLES**

1. **Use Nepali First** > Always default to Nepali (NOT Hindi)
2. **Consistency Throughout** > Everything else
3. **Natural Flow** > Literal accuracy
4. **Minimize English to <5%** > Non-negotiable

**Create a Nepali self-help book that feels originally written in Nepali—warm, consistent, and free of unnecessary English.** 📖✨
