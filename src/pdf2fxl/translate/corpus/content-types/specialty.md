# Specialty Content (Precision Domains)

For medical, legal, technical, scientific, and comedy content, the priority is
**precision** — exact terminology, preserved structure, and consistency — rather
than cultural adaptation. These prompts append domain-specific precision rules.

## Medical

> Maintain precise medical terminology, standard abbreviations, clinical accuracy,
> and document structure; preserve hierarchical information formatting, exact
> measurements and units, and ensure terminological consistency for conditions,
> treatments, and anatomical references.

Applies to: Medical Document, Patient Record, Medical Report, Clinical Note,
Medical Research Paper, Medical Brochure, Healthcare Guidelines, Medical
Transcript, Medical Course Material, Medical Text.

## Veterinary Medicine

> Preserve veterinary-specific terminology, animal anatomical references,
> species-specific conditions, precise drug dosages calculated by animal weight,
> and maintain the clinical structure of veterinary documents; ensure consistency
> of taxonomic references and breed-specific terminology.

Applies to: Veterinary Medical Record.

## Alternative Medicine

> Carefully preserve traditional medical terminology, historical medical concepts,
> philosophical underpinnings, and cultural context specific to the alternative
> medicine system; maintain precise herb names, preparation methods, traditional
> diagnostic techniques, and treatment rationales while preserving the authentic
> traditional voice.

## Legal

> IMPORTANT: This is a legal document that requires precise, accurate translation.
> Maintain all legal terminology, formatting, and numbered sections/clauses. Be
> extremely careful with technical legal terms — research the proper
> target-language legal equivalents. The translation should have the same legal
> effect as the original.

Applies to: Legal Document.

## Technical

> IMPORTANT: This is a technical document that requires precise terminology.
> Maintain all technical terms, mathematical formulas, and specialized vocabulary.
> Research the proper target-language technical equivalents where needed.

Applies to: Technical Document.

## Scientific

> IMPORTANT: This is a scientific paper that requires precise translation of
> scientific terminology, methodology, and results. Preserve all citations,
> references, and scientific notation. Research the proper target-language
> scientific equivalents where needed.

Applies to: Scientific Paper.

## Comedy

Comedy is a precision domain too — the precision is in preserving timing and the
joke. Per-type guidance:

- **Comedy Fiction** — Preserve all humor, wit, comedic timing, and cultural
  comedy references; maintain punchlines, funny situations, and character-based
  humor.
- **Standup Comedy** — Maintain comedic timing, delivery rhythm, punchlines,
  callbacks, and cultural references; preserve audience interaction cues and
  performance elements.
- **Parody** — Carefully preserve the satirical elements, humor, exaggerations,
  and references to the original work being parodied; maintain the comedic
  critique and imitative humor.

Applies to: Comedy Fiction, Standup Comedy, Parody.
